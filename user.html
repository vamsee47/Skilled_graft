Luxury Rooms

Luxury rooms, with a luxurious double bed and seating area, are popular with business travellers and families. Modern and contemporary, they may accommodate an extra bed or interconnect.

Premium Quality Rooms

A premium room is anything that the hotel deems is better than their standard, whether it is a higher floor, corner room, balcony, refrigerator, larger room, better view, etc. It means the room has more than the standard room. It does NOT mean suite

Deluxe Rooms

A single or double room is typically used for 1-2 persons travelling as the bedding configuration is normally one bed. A twin room is for 2 persons travelling however the room normally has 2 beds. A triple room accommodates up to 3 people with either one double and a single bed or a combination of beds and roll-aways.
